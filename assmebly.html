At GM Aerotech, we offer comprehensive assembly services, from installing precision inserts to handling complex sub-assemblies, tailored to meet diverse customer needs. As specialists in miniature sub-assemblies for various industries, we combine expertly manufactured components with advanced sub-assembly techniques. Our capabilities include the use of fasteners, adhesives, and welding technologies to deliver precise, high-quality results.
By sourcing sub-assemblies from us, customers can streamline their procurement process, reducing the need to manage multiple components. We provide fully finished kits, ready for further production, minimizing downtime and boosting efficiency. Whether for small production runs or large-scale OEM orders, we serve international clients of all sizes.
